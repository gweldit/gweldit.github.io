About Me

I'm an early-career machine learning researcher with experience in deep learning, generative models, and cybersecurity applications. I’m especially interested in applying ML to real-world problems in health, security, and language accessibility—particularly in data-scarce domains.

Research Interests

  • Machine Learning for Cybersecurity: malware modeling, anomaly detection
  • Generative Models: GANs, Transformers for sequence/data generation
  • Low-Resource ML: interpretable and efficient models under data scarcity
  • Computer Vision: biometric authentication, infectious disease detection
  • NLP: topic modeling, translation for African languages
  • Trustworthy AI: model robustness, privacy, and fairness
